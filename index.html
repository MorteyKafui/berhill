About Us

Berhill Services Limited is an Outsourcing and General Services provider with a commitment to deliver excellent customer service. The Company started out as a small family business in 2012, when its core businesses were Facility Management and Supplying corner grocers and small retail outlets with grocery items imported from the UK and the US. The company has grown rapidly since then and now directly employs over 150 people across the business, with its warehouse and offices in Ghana, Nigeria and the UK. The basic function of the company is to source, market and distribute Fast Moving Consumer Goods from both the domestic and overseas markets. Berhill Services currently is a major distributor for Nestlé Ghana Ltd, Willmar, Conserveria Ghana (Gino) and many more. With a monthly turnover in sales of over ¢5,000,000 in our distribution for Nestlé within our Accra sector (Ghana) and connects, we present the best blend to reach the 70-75% controlled traditional market in Ghana and West Africa at large and the 25-30% modern trade avenues.

Our strategically positioned offices, retail outlets and warehouses in Ghana and Nigeria, already puts Berhill miles ahead, given our presence in the two largest FMCG markets in West Africa. Our competitive edge includes our warehousing facilities, over 50,000 grassroots distribution channels that is coordinated by our agents and sub-agents and well established end user communication loops. Notwithstanding, our top management team of experts with a combined over 60 years’ experience in outsourcing, imports and distribution, have demonstrated capacity which can be seen in our glaring progress from a corner grocer distribution agent to a multinational logistics and distribution giant. We unarguably combine our knowledge of the consumer preferences, market dynamics and can do spirit with your quality and unmatched products to satisfy the consumers, wherever, whenever with whatever it takes
